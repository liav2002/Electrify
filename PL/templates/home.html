The best Technology

The accelerated technological development in recent decades leads to a constant increase in the rate of electricity production and consumption and greenhouse gas emissions. These trends lead to an increase in the temperature of Earth, which poses a significant challenge to humanity. Despite the constant increase in the use of renewable energies, these technologies do not allow a continuous supply of electricity and require the parallel development of energy storage capabilities. The research focused on the development of static zinc bromide batteries focusing on the composition of the solution, electrodes and materials The raw material of the system. Through characterization of the system by electrochemical, microscopic and spectroscopic analyses, a battery was developed based on a cathode of activated carbon and an anode of carbon nanotubes that present significantly higher energy densities than batteries currently on the industry. In addition, the effect of the surface structure on the capacity and performance of the battery was tested. At the same time, a computerized home system was developed for smart management of the production of electricity from solar energy and its storage with the aim of providing energy to areas without access to the electricity grid and eradicating global warming
